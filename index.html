About Us

Bull Motor Works is a leading premium automobile service center in Palladam, delivering top-tier vehicle care with precision, reliability, and affordable, value for money pricing. We combine advanced diagnostics, cutting-edge technology, and skilled technicians to ensure exceptional service quality. Trusted for our consistent performance, we provide tailored automotive solutions for every vehicle. Our association with Bosch Car Service reflects our commitment to excellence and innovation. We focus on high-performance, dependable, and customer-centric car care at the right price.

We are proud to be associated with Bosch Car Service, backed by Robert Bosch — a globally recognized German brand known for engineering excellence. This partnership reflects our commitment to delivering reliable, high-quality automotive care.
